Admissions

Undergraduate Admissions

PVPSIT offers undergraduate programs in various engineering disciplines. Admission is based on the rank obtained in the state-level entrance exam (EAMCET) and through management quota.

  • B.Tech - Computer Science Engineering (CSE)
  • B.Tech - Information Technology (IT)
  • B.Tech - Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • B.Tech - Mechanical Engineering (ME)
  • B.Tech - Civil Engineering (CE)
  • B.Tech - Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE)
  • B.Tech - Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI & ML)
  • B.Tech - Data Science (DS)

Postgraduate Admissions

The institute offers postgraduate programs for students seeking advanced knowledge and research opportunities. Admissions are based on the rank obtained in GATE/PGECET or through management quota.

  • M.Tech - Various Engineering Specializations
  • MBA - Master of Business Administration

Eligibility Criteria

For B.Tech programs:

  • Rank in EAMCET or equivalent entrance examination.
  • Pass in 10+2 or equivalent with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry as core subjects.

For M.Tech and MBA programs:

  • Rank in GATE/PGECET (for M.Tech).
  • Graduation in a relevant discipline (for M.Tech and MBA).

Application Process

To apply for admissions, follow these steps:

  • Visit the official website: www.pvpsiddhartha.ac.in
  • Fill out the online application form.
  • Submit the required documents and pay the application fee.
  • Wait for the merit list or counseling schedule.
