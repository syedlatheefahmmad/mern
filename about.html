About PVPSIT

Prasad V Potluri Siddhartha Institute of Technology, established in 1998, is a pioneering self-financed institution renowned for academic excellence and discipline. The college is sponsored by Siddhartha Academy of General and Technical Education, the premier organization that runs 18 educational institutions in diverse fields of study.

The college offers B.Tech programmes in Computer Science Engineering (CSE), CSE - Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (CSM), CSE - Data Science (CSD), Information Technology (IT), Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), Mechanical Engineering (ME), Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE), and Civil Engineering (CE). The institution also offers M.Tech programmes in ECE, ME, and MBA.

The Institution is Autonomous, approved by AICTE, permanently affiliated to JNTUK, Kakinada, and accredited by NAAC with an A+ grade. All UG programmes are accredited by NBA.

Coherent with its vision, the college is committed to quality education while promoting a sense of social responsibility. Students participate in co-curricular and extra-curricular activities and have ample opportunities for holistic personality development.

The placement cell provides career guidance, enabling students to secure excellent placements, with the highest pay package being 44 LPA. The institution has signed MOUs with 60 reputed industries to ensure industry-oriented education.
